Online Abuse and Harassment in Social Media

A Comparative Analysis

Online harassment negatively affects online participation rates and the freedom of expression. With the advent of technology, this abuse is shifted to various social media platforms online. Recent work by Amnesty International has shed light on how this problem influences the lives of women globally. In each country, 500 women were surveyed while 4000 were surveyed in total across all 8 countries.

This project aims to illustrate the prevalence and impact of online abuse/harassment among women across different countries. The countries in focus are UK, U.S., New Zealand, Spain, Poland, Denmark, Sweden, and Italy. In each country, 500 women were surveyed while 4000 were surveyed in total across all 8 countries.



In total, 911 women said they experienced online abuse or harassment and 688 of whom said they had that experience using social media.


If this problem is occuring, where is this happening? Are there differences among the different countries?
